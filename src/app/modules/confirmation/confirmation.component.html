Important details to be aware of as you go forward:

  1. At the event you will be seated with your age group, this is not meant to block you from meeting with other age groups but, to help you meet with those whom you might be most likely to match with. You will have an opportunity to meet with all participants during the social hour that follows the more scripted event that precedes it.
  2. We will release demographic information prior to the event. This will not include names or phone numbers, it is meant to help you understand the group dynamics.
  3. We expect the very best behavior from all of our participants, the spirit and the letter of the law of Islamic interpersonal behavior as modeled by our Prophet (saw).“Faith consists of more than seventy branches. And haya (modesty) is a part of faith.” (Al-Bukhari) If anyone is found to have violated this behavior code, they will be asked to leave, no refund will be issued. We expect not to have to enforce this rule.
  4. In furtherance of Islamic rules of conduct, any misrepresentation, either in registration or in person will result in termination of registration and expulsion from the event.
  5. Badge pickup locations and times will be released closer to the event. Please pay close attention to communications from the Matrimonial staff.

Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Where will the events be held?
    At a ballroom at the Hilton Anatole Hotel in Dallas, TX.
  2. Is ISNA Matrimonials held concurrently to the ISNA convention?
    Yes, it is held during the convention at the same convention location with a separate ballroom.
  3. What is the event format?
    The Matrimonial event is comprised of two parts, a speed meet event followed by a light lunch where attendees freely interact.
  4. Does ISNA provide other matrimonial services?
    ISNA has an online matrimonial service and hosts matrimonial banquets during our regional conferences that occur during the year. More information can be found on the link provided below. https://isna.net/matrimonial/
  5. Do you provide refunds if I cannot attend?
    We do not provide refunds after July 30th.
  6. How long is the event?
    We do our best to limit it to 4 hours in length.
  7. Who can I contact about my concerns?
    matrimonialintern@isna.net , We are very responsive and will do our best to accommodate you and answer your questions.
  8. What is meant by demographic information?
    Age, education, ethnic group, gender, state or nation of residence.
  9. Is there a dress code?
    Yes, business casual, formal ethnic attire, any modest attire that is clean and in good condition, most of all dress to impress. No ripped jeans or shorts please.
